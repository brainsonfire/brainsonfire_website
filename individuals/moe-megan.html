Moe Megan

Puppet Master + Community Manager

Moe grew up in a land far, far away that some refer to as Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Though surrounded by rabid sports fans (dem Stillers), she somehow spent most of her early life heavily involved in music and theater. Her singing abilities led her to enroll in Furman University, where she fell in love with the Upstate. Moe graduated from Furman in 2009 with a double-degree in Religious Studies and Music, and a concentration in South Asian Studies. Hey. They may not be the most conventionally "useful" of degrees, but dang did she learn a lot!

After college Moe moved back to the ‘burgh where she worked for a graphic design firm, made biscotti for cash and nannied some pretty cool kids. After enduring a gamut of post-collegiate existential crises, she decided to follow her love of entrepreneurialism, and took a job with a local start-up, which brought her back “dahn souf” (Pittsburghese for anywhere below the Mason-Dixon Line) to Greenville. After managing a photography studio for two years, she finally found her way to Brains on Fire.

Moe likes to learn. She loves podcasts, gardening and Joni Mitchell. A foodie at heart, she’s always game to try the weirdest thing on the menu. In her free time, Moe can be found scavenging materials for floral arrangements, hanging out with her husband-to-be and their three animals (a lab, a greyhound, and a Siamese cat), or exploring the Upstate with friends.
