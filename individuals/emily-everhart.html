Emily Everhart

Gentle Nudger + Account Executive

Find Emily Online:

  • Pinterest
  • Instagram
  • Linked In
  • Chapstick Addict
  • Middle Child
  • Dog Rescuer

Emily helps hold down the Brains on Fire fort as one of the Account Executives, which keeps her busy with navigating details, keeping clients happy and extinguishing or fanning fires as needed! Originally hired as a Community Manager, she had the privilege of working with many of Brains on Fire’s communities (Heroes in Recovery, Tinkernation, DeVry University, Wonderopolis to name a few) before fully jumping into the AE pond.

Emily’s blood runneth orange, as a Tigers’ fan and Clemson University grad. She got two degrees during her time at Clemson – a BA in Communications with a focus in Media and a BA in Modern Languages (Spanish) just for fun.

Emily came to Brains with a background in PR, public education and event planning, having mainly worked with nonprofits and government agencies in the Upstate. She channeled that eclectic experience into an ever-evolving role at Brains, and loves pinch-hitting on any project, especially the crafty ones. Pinterest addict over here!

She is an Upstate native that enjoyed a minor detour through Europe as kid, and now resides in Easley with her hubby and small pack of large dogs. Aside from constantly vacuuming up after her huskies, she also loves to cook – just ask her about Emeals. At your own risk.
