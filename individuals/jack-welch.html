Jack Welch

Media Boss
  • Broadcast Journalism Major
  • Bluegrass Picker
  • Worked in a Textile Mill
  • Played in Top 40 Bands

Jack's experience spans a few decades in this business. He is also is a self-proclaimed "homeboy." It means he grew up just down the road in Spartanburg, SC. Keeping with the theme, he earned a degree in Journalism with an emphasis in Broadcast Production from the University of South Carolina. Who knows what he did after that, but what does matter is that he joined what would one day become Brains on Fire in July of 1988.

Jack is the man. No, really. In addition to his workload as Media Boss and lending a helping hand on accounts from American Federal to Bon Secours St. Francis Health System to Visit Greenville SC, Jack holds the key to all the logistics at BOF. But when he's not here, you'll probably find him practicing with his bluegrass band. Jack Also serves on the Greenville Family Partnership board.
