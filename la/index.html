Animal Volcano Design

We’re thrilled to announce that as of January 22, 2015, Animal Volcano Design is now Brains on Fire LA. Founded in Greenville, South Carolina in 1998, Brains on Fire has built a strong reputation for developing award-winning brand identities, people-centric ambassador programs and word of mouth marketing strategies for a roster of clients that includes: Fiskars Brands, Best Buy, Love 146, The National Center for Families Learning, BMW, VisitGreenvilleSC, Foundations Recovery Network and The Environmental Defense Fund. Brains on Fire LA, the new West Coast office, will focus on human-centered design, video storytelling and digital strategies with a strong focus on meaningful user experiences.

A Beautiful Partnership

Much of AVD’s success has been the result of subscribing to the same core values and principals that Brains on Fire has championed for years. We believe movements are the most powerful and sustainable way to create real change for our clients, and we believe that people are worth giving a damn about. Over the years, we have had the opportunity to partner with Brains on Fire on several projects and mutual clients. The harmonious and collaborative working relationship between our teams made merging a natural fit and an easy decision. >

What This Means For You

So, what does this mean for you? Bottom line: bigger and better things. By combining forces, we’re now able to do and offer a lot more than either of our agencies could alone. From digital storytelling to word of mouth marketing strategy, branding and identity to ambassador program development, Brains on Fire now does it all. As a client, you’ll have some of the brightest minds in the industry (and some of the most passionate people on the planet) championing your success. We care about you. We care about what you do. And we’re here – from Carolina to California – to be your partner in making a meaningful difference in the world.
