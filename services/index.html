Our Services

So, you want to work with Brains on Fire?

Brains on Fire is, by design, a small company. We believe that a tight and lean team gives us the ability to create meaningful, artisanal-quality work while really getting to know our clients on a deep, personal level. As a result, we only take on a handful of new clients each year. Interested in working with us? Check out the service list below to explore what we do and how we do it.

Brand Identity Development

Firestarter Seminar Series

Think of the Firestarter Seminar Series as a one-day Brains on Fire boot camp geared toward empowering brands and organizations with the tools and knowledge they need to spark and sustain word of mouth movements on their own. As a participant, you can expect to bounce ideas around with the BOF team, as well as kindred spirits from a broad range of up to 20 other businesses.
