2x2 Workshop

We understand that travel can pose a challenge at times, which is precisely why we created the 2x2 Workshop. Instead of asking you to bring your team to Brains on Fire, we’ll send two of our strategic partners to you.

The 2x2 Workshop is a knowledge-sharing experience designed to impart wisdom, tools and know-how from our brains to yours. We’ll talk you through the processes we use to spark and sustain word of mouth movements, and help you develop an action plan outlining steps to take after we leave.

There’s a misguided piece of proverbial wisdom that says, “Those who can’t do…teach.” We believe that those who can do want to teach others how to do it for themselves.

For all of you budget-focused types wondering what something like this might run you, 2x2 Workshops start at $20k. Interested in scheduling a 2x2 for your team? Send an email to Robbin to get the ball rolling.
