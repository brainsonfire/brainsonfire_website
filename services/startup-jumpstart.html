Startup Jumpstart

What the heck is a Startup Jumpstart? We’re so glad you asked. The Startup Jumpstart offering is designed for startups in the incubation phase of their brand life. A Brains on Fire SWAT team will descend upon your startup for two days of intense doing. We’ll poke and prod every aspect of your business (don’t worry, we’ll be gentle), then we’ll get busy crafting your brand story, designing your identity mark (logo), shaping up a company manifesto, developing a marketing plan and designing your online look and feel. We’ll also help you develop a tight pitch designed to catch the attention and support of investors by articulating what you do and why it matters.

While there are great startups popping up left and right, we’re very selective about who we work with for Startup Jumpstart. We handpick the teams we work with, and limit ourselves to two really amazing Startup Jumpstarts per year. And since we’re sure you’re wondering what something like this might run you, let’s go ahead and get the money dance out of the way. Startup Jumpstarts begin at $5,000.

Ready to jump in? Our next Startup Jumpstart session is in March 2014. Apply today by sending us a two-minute video explaining how and why your business will change the world. We’ll grab some popcorn, give it a watch, and be in touch soon.
