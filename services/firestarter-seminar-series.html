Firestarter Seminar Series

Firestarter Seminars take place right here at the Brains on Fire headquarters in Greenville, South Carolina. Choose between one of our four seminars listed below. Times and dates to be determined. After a full day of big thinking, you’ll head back home with an action plan in hand to improve your marketing and give your movement the momentum it needs to get off the ground and grow up big and strong. If you’re in the market for additional guidance, Brains On Fire staff will be available the day following Firestarter Seminars for individual consultation at an additional cost.

Non-Profits Explore how your nonprofit can spark and sustain word of mouth movements by connecting and engaging with passionate advocates. In this session we’ll use the Passion Conversation Playbook to create an action plan your organization can implement when you head back home.

Community Management A wise BOFer once said, “Become famous for the people who love you, for the way you love them.” In this session we’ll explore why community management is about so much more than knowing how to use social media tools. Come prepared to hear from some of Brains on Fire’s seasoned community managers as they share experiences and best practices for creating meaningful connections between brands and their fans.

Startups Your story is what sets you apart from all the other brands in your space. In this storytelling boot camp, the BOF team will help startups peel back the layers of “what” and “how” to reveal the “why” that defines their core brand story. We’ll then help you determine how to share that story with others in a compelling, emotionally-engaging way.

Jumpstart Camp You got a problem? Good! We can help with that. In this session we’ll tackle your business problems, questions and quandaries, arming you with the solutions, tools and recommendations to make 2015 the best year ever.

Interested in attending? Great! Sign up for our email updates and be the first to find out when these seminars become available.
