What if you could spark deep and meaningful dialogue with the people you serve? What if you could add a volunteer sales team to your organization or rally a team of loud and proud advocates that will love you and support you in good times and in bad? What if you were FAMOUS for the people who love you…for the way YOU LOVE THEM? What if you and your team got out of bed each morning with a clear understanding that wasn’t limited to products, services and marketing goals, but encompassed the truly important things – like your cause, your purpose and your mission?

It’s possible.

At Brains on Fire, we believe that sparking conversation and creating momentum for the things you do, make or provide begins by finding and celebrating the shared passions between your company, your employees and your customers.

This is not a short-term effort; it’s hard work, but it’s also totally worth it.

If this sounds interesting, here’s what you can expect:

Phase 1: Insight During the insight phase we gather knowledge by listening to, learning about and exploring your company, offerings, market, competitors and customers. Brains on Fire staff will visit your offices to guide you through a series of interviews and exercises designed to help us become students of your business. We’ll have deep conversations with your biggest fans to learn why they love you so much and how your brand or organization fits into their lives. We’ll also explore brands, communities and experts beyond your immediate neighborhood.

Phase 2: Strategy During phase two, we will focus on developing a single strategic pathway, and then develop a detailed plan to bring your movement to life through an Ambassador-Led Community. The plan will also include components such as brand identity, engagement tools, community content recommendations, community management plan, digital direction and recommendations for success measurements.

Phase 3: Implementation Upon approval of strategy, we will enter phase three: implementation. This is where we launch your Community. Lead advocates will be selected and trained using a custom curriculum based on Brains on Fire’s community management expertise. Ongoing management of the community will be coordinated by Brains on Fire, an client internal team, or a combination of the two. The implementation phase also includes development of an online platform, as well as offline conversation tools. This is where and when conversation and passion are ignited, and the community begins to take root.

We won’t tell you this is an easy process, but if you’re feeling brave, we can tell you that this is a better way – and the results are totally worth it. We know you’re probably wondering what something like this is going to cost you, so in the spirit of transparency, let’s go ahead and get the money dance out of the way. Insight and strategy development for an Ambassador Led Movement starts at $150k, depending on the scope of the project. Implementation, which takes place over the following nine months, typically comes in around 3-4x the cost of the insight and strategy phase. Keep in mind that building community isn’t just a financial investment – it’s an investment of mind, heart and passion. In many instances, it’s a whole new way of thinking about and doing business. (And that’s a good thing!)
