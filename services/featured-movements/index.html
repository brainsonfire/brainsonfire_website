What if you could spark deep and meaningful dialogue with the people you serve? What if you could add a volunteer sales team to your organization or rally a team of loud and proud advocates that will love you and support you in good times and in bad? What if you were FAMOUS for the people who love you…for the way YOU LOVE THEM? What if you and your team got out of bed each morning with a clear understanding that wasn’t limited to products, services and marketing goals, but encompassed the truly important things – like your cause, your purpose and your mission?

It’s possible.

At Brains on Fire, we believe that sparking conversation and creating momentum for the things you do, make or provide begins by finding and celebrating the shared passions between your company, your employees and your customers.

This is not a short-term effort; it’s hard work, but it’s also totally worth it.

If this sounds interesting, here’s what you can expect:

Phase 1: Insight During the insight phase we gather knowledge by listening to, learning about and exploring your company, offerings, market, competitors and customers. Brains on Fire staff will visit your offices to guide you through a series of interviews and exercises designed to help us become students of your business. We’ll have deep conversations with your biggest fans to learn why they love you so much and how your brand or organization fits into their lives. We’ll also explore brands, communities and experts beyond your immediate neighborhood.

Phase 2: Strategy During phase two, we will focus on developing a single strategic pathway, and then develop a detailed plan to bring your movement to life through an Ambassador-Led Community. The plan will also include components such as brand identity, engagement tools, community content recommendations, digital direction and recommendations for success measurements.

Phase 3: Implementation Upon approval of strategy, we will enter phase three: implementation. This is where we launch your Community. Lead advocates will be selected and trained using a custom curriculum based on Brains on Fire’s community management expertise. The implementation phase also includes development of an online platform, as well as offline conversation tools. This is where and when conversation and passion are ignited, and the community begins to take root.

Building community isn’t just a financial investment – it’s an investment of mind, heart and passion. In many instances, it’s a whole new way of thinking about and doing business. (And that’s a good thing!) We won’t tell you it’s easy, but if you’re feeling brave, we can tell you that this is a better way — and the results are totally worth it.

If this still sounds interesting and you’d like to be one of the four Ambassador-led Community projects we take on per year, you’re in luck. We have an opening for February 2014.
Get your application in lickety-split and we’ll be in touch.
