Hops Grill and Brewery

Services Provided: Brand Identity Redesign, Collateral, Copywriting, Logo, Merchandise, Web Design, Website Development Management

Mmmmm. Beer.

Hops Grill & Brewery is known for their handcrafted microbrewing skills and some of the best steaks around. And you know how we love food (and beer), so when they asked us for a drinking hand to create names and identities for their beers – in addition to a restaurant-wide facelift, how could we refuse?

Each seasonal brew had its own unique qualities, from light and playful to dark and sophisticated, so the names and identities ran the gamut. And man-oh-man, the research was a delight!
