Charleston Parks Conservancy

Services Provided: Brand Identity Redesign, Collateral, Copywriting, Logo, Merchandise, Web Design, Website Development Management

The Charleston Parks Conservancy is a new organization with a timeless goal. They are a conduit through which Charleston’s rich horticultural history meets continued beautification. With their roots in history and their eyes on the future, they work to provide for Charleston’s parks the same level of respect and preservation that the public already gives their great city. They are on a mission to create a common goal of park preservation for every public space in Charleston as they connect people to people, people to the past and people to their parks.
