Ryobi

Services Provided: Brand Identity Redesign, Collateral, Copywriting, Logo, Merchandise, Web Design, Website Development Management

So, through our Insight, we crawled into the head of the do-it-yourself-er and came out the other end with a name and icon that was simple and easily recognizable in a wall full of power tools.

Three weeks after launch, we were told by Ryobi HQ that sales were 10 times what they had projected!
