Gadow Handcrafted Guitars

Services Provided: Brand Identity Design, Collateral, Copywriting, Logo, Brochure, Web Design

We can’t say Ryan Gadow is passionate about making handcrafted guitars. Because that word doesn’t do him justice. Ryan is fanatical about every single minuscule detail that goes into each of his guitars and basses. And his company needed an identity that not only reflected his commitment to perfection, but spoke to that specific sliver of the musical instrument community that can not only appreciate his work, but make his work sing, too.

And here’s where we landed:
Gadow is taking music back to its roots. Great American music has always been a grass roots phenomenon. It’s about jam sessions held in garages, basements, smoky roadhouses and country churches. It’s raw, authentic, honest and real. At Gadow, that’s what we respect. And that’s why we do what we do.
