How it all works

We’ve spoken at conferences (big and small) spreading passion, inspiration, and oodles of marketing know-how around the globe. Our speakers can deliver presentations or workshops on how to spark love and passion from your biggest advocates as well as how to ignite powerful, meaningful, sustainable word of mouth movements.

Our standard speaking fee is $2,500 + 100* books + travel expenses. If you’re interested in bringing a little of the Brains on Fire magic to your event, contact Amy to get the ball rolling. Act fast, because this offer won’t last long. Our speaking schedule fills up quickly and we have a limited number of dates available. (Client work always comes first in our world!)
